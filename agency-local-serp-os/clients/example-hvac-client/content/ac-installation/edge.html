When should I replace my AC unit in Florida?

In Florida, replace your AC unit when it reaches 10–15 years old, requires R-22 refrigerant, or needs a repair costing more than half the price of a new system. Constant breakdowns, rising power bills, and uneven cooling are the clearest signs. Florida's year-round heat ages units faster, so plan ahead of summer.
