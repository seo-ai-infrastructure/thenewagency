## How much does an AC refrigerant recharge cost in Fort Lauderdale?

An AC refrigerant recharge in Fort Lauderdale costs **$250 to $650**, depending on refrigerant type and how much your system lost. ## Why AC repair costs what it does in Fort Lauderdale

South Florida AC systems run nearly 12 months a year, so components like capacitors, contactors, and compressors wear faster than in cooler climates. Coastal Broward County adds **salt-air corrosion** that eats at electrical contacts and condenser coils. Older homes still running phased-out **R-22 refrigerant** face the steepest recharge costs. The upside: catching small issues early — a $200 capacitor instead of a $2,000 compressor — keeps your Fort Lauderdale repair bill low.

## How to avoid overpaying for AC repair

- **Get a flat-rate quote, not hourly.** You should approve a price before work starts.
- **Insist on a leak check before any recharge.** Refilling without fixing the leak wastes money.
- **Ask if the diagnostic fee is credited** toward the repair (ours is).
- **Beware "compressor scares."** Get a second opinion before approving a $2,000+ part.
- **Keep up with tune-ups.** A maintained system fails less and costs less to repair.
